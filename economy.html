Economy in Nantes

Nantes biggest companies are SNCF, Bouygues Telecom and CapGemini Nantes economy use to be linked to the Loire and the Atlantic, the city had Frances largest harbour in the 18th century. Food processing predominated during the Industrial Age, with sugar refineries, biscuit factories, canned fish and processed vegetables; these brands still dominate the French market. The Nantes region is Frances largest food producer

Nantes experienced deindustrialisation after port activity in Saint-Nazaire largely ceased, culminating in the 1987 closure of the shipyards. The city attempted to attract service firms. CapGemini, SNCF and Bouygues Telecom opened offices in the city. Since 2000 Nantes has had one of the biggest financial district in France and is the third largest financial centre in France after Paris and Lyon
