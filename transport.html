Public Transport

There is a lot of public transport in Nantes from trams to buses all managed by the Nantes Metropole. There are 3 tram lines throughout the city providing an easy way to explore the city for a cheap price. The bus routes are also an effortless way to explore everywhere from the city centre to the estates to the hotels. The public transport has one unified way of paying with the Pass Nantes where you can pay for all public transport and has added bonuses as well such as free museum tickets.
