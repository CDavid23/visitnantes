Population

Currently, the population of Nantes is 283,025 people with a population density of 4,341.54 people for every square km. Nantes's 2024 population is now estimated at 705,609. In 1950, the population of Nantes was 276,290. Nantes has grown by 6,281 in the last year, which represents a 0.9% annual change The city of Nantes covers considerable ground in terms of surface area. The city proper contains 65.19 kilometers squared.The metropolitan area expands out to a total of 3,302 kilometers squared. The population density of the residents in the city proper comes to a total of 4,700 living per square kilometre , while the metropolitan density drops to about 280 residents per square kilometres.
